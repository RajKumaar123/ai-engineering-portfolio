# 🤖 Agentic AI

Agentic AI represents the next evolution of Artificial Intelligence, where autonomous systems can reason, plan, make decisions, use external tools, collaborate with other agents, and execute complex multi-step workflows with minimal human intervention.

Unlike traditional AI applications that respond to a single prompt, Agentic AI systems continuously perceive, reason, act, learn, and adapt to achieve defined objectives. These systems combine Large Language Models (LLMs), memory, planning, retrieval, tool usage, and orchestration to solve real-world problems autonomously.

This section provides a comprehensive guide to designing, building, evaluating, and deploying production-grade Agentic AI systems.
